About me

Personal

As mentioned in the not so subtle headline, my name is Brian. I live in Maarslet - a small town south of Aarhus, complete with green fields and croaking frogs - with my girlfriend Janni, our cat and (mostly my) three four geckos (I recently hatched my first baby gecko!).

When I am not working I play basketball, computer games and read fantasy books. I also tend to my geckos and read about all the cool animals around the world. Last but not least, I (of course) read about the latest online trends.

Work

In my humble opinion, designing for the web requires more than just design skills. You at least know your way around the basics of web development. So apart from my design skills, I also consider myself as a somewhat heavy frontend developer and UX expert.

My usual workflow (assuming the project has some kind of corporate identity) starts out with some sketching on paper and basic design in Fireworks. As soon as possible I move to HTML and CSS to create some quick working prototypes. From there it is user testing, design, development and rinse and repeat.

I work agile, create mobile first responsive websites, I got an expert eye for details and create accessible websites with focus on the users experience.
